ABOUT

Welcome to Walmart, the world's largest retailer and a one-stop-shop for all your shopping needs. We are committed to helping our customers save money and live better, offering a wide range of products at affordable prices.

Our History:
Walmart was founded in 1962 by Sam Walton in Rogers, Arkansas. Since then, we have grown to become the largest retailer in the world, serving millions of customers every day. We are proud of our heritage and are dedicated to providing the best shopping experience to our customers.

Our Values:
At Walmart, we believe in providing value to our customers, respecting individuals, serving communities, striving for excellence, and acting with integrity. These values guide us in everything we do and help us create a positive impact on the world around us.

Our Products:
We offer a wide range of products, including groceries, household essentials, electronics, apparel, and more. Our stores are designed to provide a convenient shopping experience, and we constantly strive to improve our selection and quality to meet the changing needs of our customers.

Our Services:
In addition to our products, we offer a variety of services to make your shopping experience more convenient. These services include free pickup and delivery, pharmacy services, and financial services.

Our Commitment to Sustainability:
We are committed to sustainability and are working to reduce our environmental impact through various initiatives, such as reducing waste, conserving energy, and sourcing sustainable products. We also support local communities through our philanthropic efforts and partnerships with organizations that promote sustainability and social responsibility.

Thank you for choosing Walmart. We are dedicated to providing you with the best shopping experience and look forward to serving you in our stores and online.
